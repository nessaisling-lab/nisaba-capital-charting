## Pre-record checklist

- [ ] AAPL data fully loaded (run scraper once, then close it — keeps DB warm)

## Production tips

- **Practice the script TWICE before recording.** Times will drift on the first pass.
- **The 3-second pause after the J.P. Morgan quote is non-negotiable.** Let it land.
- **If you flub a line, restart that section — don't try to splice mid-sentence.** Cleaner.
- **Voice tone:** confident, slightly slow. Not "selling." Demonstrating.
- **Don't apologize for astrology, even subtly.** The historical precedent does the convincing.
- **End hard on "that engine."** Don't soften with "I hope you enjoyed" or "thanks for watching."

## Cuts to make if running long

If the demo runs over 3 minutes:
1. **First cut:** Section 4 fetch animation aside. Skip from "10 native Rust scrapers" straight to "10 native scrapers replacing OpenBB" → next tab.
2. **Second cut:** Section 5 backtest. Just say "the backtest engine has a cycle-aligned mode that filters for Saturn-return years" without clicking through.
3. **Last cut:** Reduce Section 6 numbers to "30 days, 140 tests, zero warnings."

## Cuts to make if running short (under 2:30)

Add to Section 4:
- Open Research tab, scroll past 25 RSS sources + GDELT events + Polymarket odds.
- "Research isn't a side panel. It's a full intelligence-aggregation layer."
